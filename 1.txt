Dear BJP, why do you hate Delhi, AAP asks as Centre 'blocks' ration scheme.
Centre's permission is not required to launch such a scheme under the existing rules, Arvind Kejriwal's office said on Saturday after its Ghar Ghar Ration Yojana Scheme got stalled by L-G Anil Baijal.The Aam Aadmi Party on Saturday accused the Centre of stalling Delhi's doorstep delivery of ration scheme, which was scheduled to begin with one or two days. The scheme would have benefitted 72 lakh people in Delhi.
